## Using the Marketplace

### Animal Feed (Dry Bread) Category
**NEW**: Special category connecting bakeries with livestock farmers!

**Benefits:**
- Bakeries: Sell unsold dry bread profitably
- Farmers: Get affordable animal feed

## Troubleshooting

### Map Not Loading
- Check internet connection
- Zoom out on map view
- Refresh page (Ctrl+R)
- Clear browser cache

### Voice Not Working
- Check browser permissions (Microphone)
- Ensure sound is ON
- Try Chrome/Firefox (best support)
- Check Settings > Voice toggle

### Neighborhood Not Found
- Try full neighborhood name
- Check spelling (Dely Ibrahim, not Dely Ibrahime)
- Use "Popular Neighborhoods" section
- Zoom in on map for more details

### Payment Issues
- Clear cookies & login again
- Try different payment method
- Contact support: hello@looprefood.dz

## FAQ

**Q: Is Loop Refood available outside Algiers?**
A: Currently Algiers only. Expansion to Oran, Constantine coming Q2 2024.

**Q: How fresh is the food?**
A: All items are 1-24 hours old. Expiry times shown for each item.

**Q: Can I become a seller?**
A: Yes! Register as Business. Requirements: Food license, insurance, 4.5+ rating.

**Q: How are prices set?**
A: Sellers choose prices (Loops suggests 40-70% off). No markup fees.

**Q: Is there a referral program?**
A: Yes! Get 200 DZD per friend signup + 5% of their first 5 purchases.

**Q: Can I donate instead of buying?**
A: Yes! Partner with charities (coming soon). Donate surplus or buy & donate.

**Q: How do I track my CO2 impact?**
A: Badge auto-calculates per purchase. 1 kg food = 2.1 kg CO2 prevented.
