🍎 ReFood Web App
ReFood is a high-end, AI-powered Full-Stack web application designed to combat food waste and optimize food redistribution. It creates a seamless ecosystem between food donors (restaurants, hotels, and grocery stores) and beneficiaries (charities or individuals) through an intelligent, data-driven platform.

🚀 Key Features
👥 Dual-User Ecosystem
Donor Dashboard: Allows businesses to list surplus food, track their charitable impact, and manage donation schedules in real-time.

Recipient/Charity Portal: Enables organizations to browse available food near them, claim donations, and coordinate logistics for pickup.

🗺️ Intelligent Logistics & Mapping
Real-time Geo-Tracking: Integration of interactive maps to locate the nearest available food batches.

Expiry-First Logic: AI-sorted listings that prioritize food items closest to their expiration date to ensure zero waste.

💰 Impact & Sustainability Tracking
Carbon Offset Calculator: Users can see how many CO2 emissions were saved by preventing food from going to landfills.

Donation Certificates: Generates digital reports for businesses to use for tax incentives and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).

🚛 Smart Redistribution Service
Volunteer/Driver Coordination: A specialized module where independent drivers can accept "Delivery Tasks" by scanning QR Codes to confirm food safety and transfer of custody.

📊 Advanced Analytics for Food Security
Supply-Demand Visualizer: Graphical representation of food types (Cooked, Fresh, Canned, Bakery) and their distribution trends.

Waste Prevention Insights: Analytical tools for donors to identify over-production patterns and reduce future costs.

🛠️ Technical Stack
Frontend: React.js, Tailwind CSS (Ultra-Professional UI).

Backend/Database: Supabase (Real-time data synchronization & Secure Auth).

Internationalization (i18n): Full Multi-language support (Arabic/English) with seamless RTL/LTR layout switching.

Typography: 'Tajawal' for a premium Arabic user experience.

🎨 Design Philosophy
The app follows a fresh, modern aesthetic with a primary focus on the brand identity color #FF6B6B (Appetite-Red) or #4CAF50 (Sustainability-Green). It features smooth UI transitions, a high-impact Splash Screen, and a fully responsive layout.

🧠 ReFood: AI & Data Analytics Architecture
ReFood leverages a sophisticated Hybrid Intelligence Framework to transform food surplus data into actionable social impact.

1. Predictive Food Analytics
Shelf-Life Optimization: The system employs algorithms to categorize food batches based on perishability. It uses Time-Sensitive Analytics to alert nearby charities before food becomes unsafe for consumption.

Demand Forecasting: By analyzing historical donation data, the AI predicts "Hunger Zones" or areas with high food demand, helping donors allocate resources more effectively.

2. Intelligent Geospatial Logistics (Logistics AI)
Cold-Chain Optimization: For the pickup service, the platform calculates the fastest routes to ensure fresh food reaches its destination within safety windows, minimizing transport time.

Proximity Matching: Real-time algorithms link donors to the closest verified charities to reduce the logistics footprint.

3. Verification & Food Safety (Trust Logic)
Smart Verification: The system incorporates QR-Code Handshaking for every transaction, ensuring that food is handed over to authorized personnel only.

Compliance Tracking: Digitally logs food handling certifications and donor ratings to maintain a high standard of food safety and trust.

4. Business Intelligence (BI) for Donors
Tax Benefit Reporting: Donors have access to a BI Dashboard that tracks the monetary value of donated food, providing automated reports for government tax deductions and sustainability audits.
